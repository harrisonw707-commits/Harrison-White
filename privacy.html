Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2024

1. Information We Collect

HireQuest AI is designed as a "privacy-first" application. We collect:

  • Microphone Audio: Used solely during live sessions for real-time AI interaction. Audio is streamed to Google Gemini API for transcription and is not stored by HireQuest.
  • Local Data: Your interview history and resumes are stored locally on your device (Local Storage) and never uploaded to our servers.

2. AI Processing

We use the Google Gemini API to process your interview answers. Your interactions are subject to Google's Generative AI Privacy Policy. We do not use your data to train our own models.

3. Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by using the "Clear All History" button within the application settings or by clearing your browser's cache/local storage.

4. Contact Us

For any questions regarding this policy, please contact us via our developer website.
